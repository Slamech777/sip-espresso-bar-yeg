Our Story

A café on two wheels

SIP Espresso Bar brings the warmth of a neighbourhood café straight to your event. Our mobile coffee bike rolls up wherever you need it — patios, weddings, markets, offices — and our baristas pull every shot to order.

No stale coffee pots. No compromise. Just rich espresso, velvety steamed milk, and the kind of hospitality that turns a quick cup into a memorable moment.
